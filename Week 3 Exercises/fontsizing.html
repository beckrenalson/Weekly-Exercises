px: pixels (1px = 1/96th of 1in) are relative to the viewing device. For low-dpi devices, 1px is one device pixel (dot) of the display. For printers and high resolution screens 1px implies multiple device pixels.

pt: points (1pt = 1/72 of 1in)

em: Relative to the font-size of the element (2em means 2 times the size of the current font)

rem: Relative to font-size of the root element
